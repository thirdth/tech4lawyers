Ethics of Security -- making sure your data are secure

Introduction

In what I consider a delayed response to the market, many states, including my own are now adopting rule of conduct designed to keep pace with ever-changing technology and the business practices that come from that.

Since most of the rule changes are crafted in accord with the ABA Model Rules, we will use those as our guide, however, your state may or may not have adopted these changes at all, or may have adopted them in a modified way. As always, go to your relevant legal source.
